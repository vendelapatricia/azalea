Placement

Avoid placing flower arrangements in windowsills and other areas with full sun where flowers can wilt due to overheating.

Water

Water your plants often, but not to ooften. Feel the soil and only water if it is dry. Some plants need more water than others, learn about your species.

Withered leaves

Remove excess leaves. Remove leaves, flowers, or branches which would fall below the water line. Having these in the water encourages bacteria growth.
